Employee Onboarding System


The Business Problem

HR teams lose track of new hire onboarding when it's managed through emails and spreadsheets. Tasks get missed, managers forget to approve, and nobody knows where things stand until the new hire shows up on day one with nothing set up.


Solution & Approach

Built a structured onboarding system where HR fills out one guided form, five checklist tasks are created automatically, the manager gets an approval request, and a live progress tracker shows exactly what's done and what isn't — all without anyone having to remember to do any of it.


What Was Built
  • → Guided onboarding form (Screen Flow)
  • → Auto-generated task checklist (5 tasks)
  • → Manager approval workflow with email alerts
  • → Live progress tracker built with LWC
  • → Role-based access — HR, Manager, Director
  • → Reports and dashboard for HR leadership

Outcome & Impact

HR now creates a complete onboarding record in under two minutes. Tasks are tracked automatically, manager approvals happen without chasing, and the progress bar updates in real time as each task is checked off.



Technology
  • Apex
  • Flow Builder
  • LWC
  • Approval Process
  • SOQL
